SERVICES

No two projects are the same, so at Philpotts we provide multiple services to best serve our clients.

CUSTOM FURNITURE

We create custom furniture and furnishings specifically designed for each individual client and environment. Creating "one of a kind" pieces for an individual residence, or for a lobby of a hotel, is an integral and unique aspect of our design services.

RESIDENTIAL DESIGN

Creating a home that represents the owner's needs, wants and aesthetics has always been the driving design philosophy at Philpotts Interiors. Weaving cultural and environmental integrity into the exterior and interior physical spaces provides core values that guide the owner and designer.

COMMERCIAL DESIGN

Whether we are renovating a bank, consulting on a master plan for a major health care institution, or working closely with a small business owner on tenant improvements, our holistic approach incorporates the entire customer experience as well as the owner’s goals.

HOSPITALITY DESIGN

Our success in each project is based on the colla-boration between owners, architects and designers. By creating a cohesive product that provides unforgettable guest experiences, we create hotels with lasting value that delight and enchant.

GREEN DESIGN

Creating spaces that make use of their natural surroundings and attributes is an important part of any design, but our team goes the extra step to use sustainable materials that incorporate those attributes without compromising on the perfor-mance, quality, and functionality each each item.
